**Polap** is a minimalist web application that brings the nostalgic charm of instant film to your browser. Capture the moment, choose your layout, and download high-quality Polaroid-style photo strips in seconds.



## âœ¨ Features

- **Dynamic Templates:** Choose from 6 different layouts, ranging from single classic squares to 4-slot vertical strips.
- **Real-time Camera:** Live webcam feed with mirroring for a natural "photo booth" experience.
- **Smart Cropping:** Custom "Object-Fit" logic ensures your photos are perfectly centered and cropped regardless of the template shape.
- **High-Res Export:** Download your creations as high-quality PNGs with a single click.
- **Audio Feedback:** Real camera shutter sound effects for an immersive feel.
